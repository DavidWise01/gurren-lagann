> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **ENTERTAINMENT**.

GURREN LAGANN (GUR) — the LINEAGE ROOT of everything Trigger: Gainax 2007, dir. Hiroyuki Imaishi, written by Kazuki Nakashima (the team that became Studio Trigger). A timid underground digger, Simon, and his hot-blooded brother Kamina break to the surface and spiral up — through a tyrant, the moon, galaxies — on willpower and ever-larger drills (Spiral Power). Kamina's death forces Simon to grow; the Anti-Spiral would freeze all growth to prevent apocalypse; the finale's quiet is the thesis. TROPES: growth/grief/inherited courage, evolution-vs-stagnation, the drill as will. Render-not-invent + IP-clean. LIVE spiral-power interactive. Vellum (no black) + drill-orange accent + quorum-ring favicon. 9 emergents.
